Brand Overview
MuscleBlaze is an Indian sports nutrition & bodybuilding supplement brand founded in 2012. It operates under Bright LifeCare Private Limited and is headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana, India. The brand targets fitness enthusiasts, athletes, and bodybuilders with a wide range of performance-oriented nutritional products designed for the Indian market.

Brand Identity & Positioning
MuscleBlaze positions itself as India’s #1 online sports nutrition and bodybuilding supplement brand focused on authenticity, performance, and consumer trust. The narrative is built around solving specific problems faced by Indian fitness consumers: access to authentic products, formulation tailored to Indian physiology/diet, and affordability compared with imported brands.

Core positioning tagline from company sources: India’s leading sports nutrition brand fueling “Ziddis” — a cultural cue implying persistence, grit, determination and relentless self-improvement.

Emphasis on “Made for India” positioning, underlying local understanding and customized formulation rather than generic global products.

Product Focus & Portfolio
MuscleBlaze’s products span the entire supplement use-case spectrum for fitness goals:

Proteins & Whey Supplements – Whey concentrate, whey isolate, Biozyme (clinically researched enhanced absorption) series.

Mass Gainers & Weight Management Supplements – For muscle gain and weight control.

Pre- & Post-Workout Products – BCAA, creatine, energy boosters.

Vitamins & Wellness – Multivitamins, fish oil, immunity whey with herbs like ashwagandha.

High-Protein Snack Foods & Accessories – Protein bars, protein peanut butter, apparel and gym accessories appear in their product ecosystem via the official app and website.

The portfolio ranges from basic protein powders to advanced, clinically researched formulations designed to aid performance, recovery, strength, and endurance goals.

Brand Values & Narrative
MuscleBlaze’s narrative centers on authenticity, innovation, quality, and accessibility.

Authenticity is reinforced through mechanisms like:

SMS-based product authentication codes to fight fake products.

Lab tests and external certifications (e.g., Labdoor for protein purity, historically).

Innovation examples include:

Clinically tested formulas (e.g., Biozyme series with enhanced absorption).

Scoop-on-Top packaging feature to solve the common scoop-search issue.

New product types blending traditional/ayurvedic ingredients (ashwagandha-enriched products).

Brand value messaging emphasizes empowering consumers to take ownership of their fitness journeys (“Zidd” theme), positioning MuscleBlaze as not just a supplement maker but a fitness partner.

Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)
These are the key factors setting MuscleBlaze apart:

India-centric formulations — products made for Indian dietary needs and training styles.

First-to-market innovations in India (SMS authenticity checks, licensed packaging innovations).

Clinically tested products (Biozyme with enhanced protein absorption claims).

Wide product range covering all major fitness goals and customer segments.

Strong community presence via campaigns, influencer partnerships, digital content, and fitness-centric brand messaging.

Brand Assets & Visual Identity (Colours)
Based on public brand repositories and identity asset sites:

Primary Brand Colour: Amber/Yellow #FFBE00 — strong highlight colour used in logo/packaging.

Secondary/Supporting Colour: White #FFFFFF as a neutral background colour with the amber used for emphasis.

Additional inferred darker tones are sometimes used (black/dark backgrounds for contrast in product packaging and digital creative), though not official hex codes.

These colours support a high-contrast, energetic palette that signals strength, energy and visibility in a fitness context.

Brand Voice & Messaging Style
MuscleBlaze’s voice tends to be bold, motivational, and aspirational — often using hard-edged fitness language and cultural terms that resonate with gym and athletic communities (e.g., “Ziddi”, “Fuel Your Grind”). This carries over into campaigns, product pages and social media messaging.

Consumer Position & Market Presence

Market leader in India’s sports nutrition category with broad digital footprint.

Wide distribution through the brand’s own website, e-commerce retailers, and offline retail channels.

Strong social, influencer and ambassador associations (e.g., partnerships with fitness personalities)

container-scroll-animation.tsx
"use client";
import React, { useRef } from "react";
import { useScroll, useTransform, motion, MotionValue } from "framer-motion";

export const ContainerScroll = ({
  titleComponent,
  children,
}: {
  titleComponent: string | React.ReactNode;
  children: React.ReactNode;
}) => {
  const containerRef = useRef<HTMLDivElement>(null);
  const { scrollYProgress } = useScroll({
    target: containerRef,
  });
  const [isMobile, setIsMobile] = React.useState(false);

  React.useEffect(() => {
    const checkMobile = () => {
      setIsMobile(window.innerWidth <= 768);
    };
    checkMobile();
    window.addEventListener("resize", checkMobile);
    return () => {
      window.removeEventListener("resize", checkMobile);
    };
  }, []);

  const scaleDimensions = () => {
    return isMobile ? [0.7, 0.9] : [1.05, 1];
  };

  const rotate = useTransform(scrollYProgress, [0, 1], [20, 0]);
  const scale = useTransform(scrollYProgress, [0, 1], scaleDimensions());
  const translate = useTransform(scrollYProgress, [0, 1], [0, -100]);

  return (
    <div
      className="h-[60rem] md:h-[80rem] flex items-center justify-center relative p-2 md:p-20"
      ref={containerRef}
    >
      <div
        className="py-10 md:py-40 w-full relative"
        style={{
          perspective: "1000px",
        }}
      >
        <Header translate={translate} titleComponent={titleComponent} />
        <Card rotate={rotate} translate={translate} scale={scale}>
          {children}
        </Card>
      </div>
    </div>
  );
};

export const Header = ({ translate, titleComponent }: any) => {
  return (
    <motion.div
      style={{
        translateY: translate,
      }}
      className="div max-w-5xl mx-auto text-center"
    >
      {titleComponent}
    </motion.div>
  );
};

export const Card = ({
  rotate,
  scale,
  children,
}: {
  rotate: MotionValue<number>;
  scale: MotionValue<number>;
  translate: MotionValue<number>;
  children: React.ReactNode;
}) => {
  return (
    <motion.div
      style={{
        rotateX: rotate,
        scale,
        boxShadow:
          "0 0 #0000004d, 0 9px 20px #0000004a, 0 37px 37px #00000042, 0 84px 50px #00000026, 0 149px 60px #0000000a, 0 233px 65px #00000003",
      }}
      className="max-w-5xl -mt-12 mx-auto h-[30rem] md:h-[40rem] w-full border-4 border-[#6C6C6C] p-2 md:p-6 bg-[#222222] rounded-[30px] shadow-2xl"
    >
      <div className=" h-full w-full  overflow-hidden rounded-2xl bg-gray-100 dark:bg-zinc-900 md:rounded-2xl md:p-4 ">
        {children}
      </div>
    </motion.div>
  );
};
